About move2U

Where every heartbeat counts — on the trail and in love.

Our Story

move2U was born from a simple but powerful idea: what if finding love was as natural as tying your running shoes on a Saturday morning? We believe that shared movement creates shared moments — and those moments can spark something extraordinary. Our platform brings together people who love staying active and who are open to meeting someone special along the way.

Every weekend, groups of like-minded people join activities — running circuits in the park, cycling scenic routes, conquering hiking trails, taking leisurely walks, or letting their dogs set the social agenda at pet-friendly meet-ups. There are no awkward dinner silences here. Just fresh air, endorphins, and the genuine version of yourself that only comes out when you're in motion.

Whether you find your soulmate or your new favourite Sunday crew, move2U guarantees one thing: you will leave better than you arrived. Healthier, happier, more connected. Because even if love doesn't show up on the trail today, fitness always will — and that's a relationship that will never let you down. 💛
